

## Plan: Add Ghana as a New Country with 5 Assessment Modules

✅ COMPLETED

Ghana has been added with the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) and five assessment modules:
1. Application of the Law (Applicability)
2. Registration with the Data Protection Commission
3. Understanding Data Subject Rights
4. Data Breach Notification
5. Appointment of Data Protection Supervisor (DPO)
